Networking & Wiring

Structured cabling and network optimization.

The structured wiring services are the most common type of networking installation. The cables that make up a structured cabling system can be made from copper, fiber optic or coaxial cable and they all have specific characteristics which determine how well they will perform under different conditions. A properly installed structured cabling system is designed to provide reliable connectivity for your business’s data communications needs. It also helps you avoid costly downtime due to equipment failure by providing redundancy so if one part fails another takes over seamlessly.
